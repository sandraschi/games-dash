üêç Snake - Learn & Strategy

The classic growing snake game

üìú The Rules

Objective: Control a snake to eat food and grow as long as possible without hitting walls or yourself.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys to control the snake's direction
  • Eat the food (colored square) to grow longer
  • Each food eaten increases your score and length
  • Game ends if you hit a wall or your own body
  • Try to achieve the highest score possible!

Scoring

  • Each food eaten: +10 points
  • Score increases with snake length
  • Some versions give bonus points for speed

üéØ Strategy Tips

Basic Strategy

  • Plan your path: Think ahead to avoid trapping yourself
  • Use the edges: Travel along walls to create space
  • Spiral pattern: Create a spiral pattern to maximize space
  • Don't chase food blindly: Make sure you have an escape route

Advanced Techniques

  • Box strategy: Create boxes and fill them systematically
  • Corner management: Use corners efficiently - don't get stuck
  • Speed control: Slow down when needed to plan moves
  • Pattern recognition: Learn common patterns that work

üìö History

Snake originated in the 1970s as a computer game. The most famous version was on Nokia phones in the 1990s, making it one of the most played games in history.

Evolution

Snake has evolved from simple arcade games to complex versions with power-ups, multiple snakes, and various game modes. It remains a classic casual game.

World Records

Perfect games (filling the entire board) are possible on smaller grids. World records exist for maximum length achieved on various grid sizes.

Change all the cubes!

üìú The Rules

Objective: Jump on all cubes to change their color. Avoid enemies and don't fall off the pyramid!

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys to jump Q*bert diagonally to adjacent cubes
  • Each cube changes color when you jump on it
  • Change all cubes to the target color to complete the level
  • Avoid enemies (Coily, Ugg, Wrong-Way, Slick, Sam)
  • Don't fall off the pyramid - you'll lose a life!

Enemies

  • Coily: Purple snake that chases you - most dangerous!
  • Ugg & Wrong-Way: Move in fixed patterns
  • Slick & Sam: Change cubes back - use discs to eliminate

üéØ Strategy Tips

Basic Strategy

  • Work systematically: Change cubes in a pattern
  • Use discs: Jump on discs to escape Coily and eliminate enemies
  • Watch enemies: Learn enemy movement patterns
  • Stay mobile: Keep moving to avoid being trapped

Advanced Techniques

  • Disc strategy: Use discs strategically to eliminate Coily
  • Corner safety: Corners can be safe or dangerous - know which
  • Pattern recognition: Learn efficient cube-changing patterns
  • Enemy timing: Time your moves to avoid enemies

üìö History

Q*bert was created by Gottlieb and released in 1982. It was one of the first games with an isometric 3D perspective.

Unique Features

Q*bert's isometric pyramid and diagonal movement were innovative for the time. The game's quirky character and sound effects made it memorable.

Legacy

Q*bert has appeared in various forms over the years and remains a cult classic. The character has appeared in other games and media.

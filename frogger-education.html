üìú The Rules

Objective: Guide your frog safely across the road and river to reach home. Avoid cars and don't fall in the water!

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys to move the frog one square at a time
  • Road section: Avoid moving cars - they'll squish you!
  • River section: Jump on logs and turtles to cross - don't fall in water!
  • Reach all 5 home spots to complete the level
  • Each level gets faster and more challenging

Scoring

  • Reaching home: 10 points √ó remaining time bonus
  • Bonus points for reaching home with time remaining
  • Extra lives every 20,000 points

üéØ Strategy Tips

Road Crossing

  • Watch patterns: Cars move in predictable patterns
  • Wait for gaps: Don't rush - wait for safe openings
  • Use medians: Stop in the middle to plan next move
  • Timing: Learn the timing of each lane

River Crossing

  • Jump on logs: Logs move - time your jumps
  • Turtle timing: Turtles dive - don't be on them when they submerge!
  • Alligator danger: Avoid alligators in some versions
  • Current awareness: Logs move at different speeds

Advanced Techniques

  • Pattern memorization: Learn traffic patterns
  • Efficient routing: Plan your path before starting
  • Time management: Balance speed with safety
  • Home strategy: Fill homes from outside in

üìö History

Frogger was created by Konami and released in 1981. It became one of the most popular arcade games of the early 1980s.

Cultural Impact

Frogger has appeared in movies, TV shows, and has been referenced in popular culture. It's considered one of the classic arcade games.

Modern Versions

Frogger has been remade for modern platforms with updated graphics, new obstacles, and various game modes, but the core gameplay remains the same.

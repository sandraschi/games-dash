The classic arcade maze game

üìú The Rules

Objective: Eat all the dots in the maze while avoiding the ghosts. Eat power pellets to turn the tables!

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys to move Pac-Man through the maze
  • Eat all dots (pellets) to complete the level
  • Avoid ghosts - they'll cost you a life!
  • Eat power pellets (large dots) to make ghosts vulnerable
  • Eat vulnerable ghosts for bonus points
  • Complete all levels to win!

Scoring

  • Regular dot: 10 points
  • Power pellet: 50 points
  • Vulnerable ghost: 200, 400, 800, 1600 points (increasing)
  • Fruit bonus: 100-5000 points (varies by level)

üéØ Strategy Tips

Basic Strategy

  • Know the maze: Learn the layout and shortcuts
  • Use corners: Ghosts slow down on corners - use this to escape
  • Save power pellets: Use them strategically, not immediately
  • Watch ghost patterns: Each ghost behaves differently

Advanced Techniques

  • Ghost manipulation: Lead ghosts away from areas you need to clear
  • Power pellet timing: Activate when ghosts are close together for maximum points
  • Tunnel strategy: Use side tunnels to escape or confuse ghosts
  • Pattern memorization: Learn safe routes through each level

üìö History

Pac-Man was created by Toru Iwatani and released by Namco in 1980. It became one of the most successful arcade games of all time, creating a cultural phenomenon.

Cultural Impact

Pac-Man was the first video game to appeal to both men and women, breaking the stereotype that arcade games were only for boys. It spawned cartoons, merchandise, and countless sequels.

World Records

The perfect Pac-Man score is 3,333,360 points (all 256 levels completed). Only a few players have achieved this legendary feat!

## Core Mechanic: Match-3 with Gravity

### Basic Concept
1. Grid of colored gems/candies/objects (8√ó8 or 10√ó10)
2. Swap adjacent pieces
3. Match 3+ in a row (horizontal/vertical)
4. Matched pieces disappear
5. Pieces above drop down (gravity!)
6. New pieces spawn at top
7. Cascading combos possible!
8. Score points, reach target, repeat forever...

### Why It's Addictive
- **Simple to learn** (swap, match, repeat)
- **Immediate feedback** (satisfying animations)
- **Variable rewards** (sometimes big combos!)
- **Just one more** (always another move possible)
- **Progression** (levels, upgrades, achievements)
- **No penalty for failure** (try again immediately)

**Psychology**: Exploits dopamine reward cycle!

## Game 1: Gem Cascade (Basic Demonstration)

### Core Features

**Grid**: 8√ó8 board  
**Pieces**: 6 colored gems (red, blue, green, yellow, purple, orange)  
**Goal**: Reach target score before moves run out  

**Mechanics**:
- **Swap**: Click two adjacent gems to swap
- **Match**: 3+ in row/column disappear
- **Drop**: Gems fall down to fill gaps
- **Spawn**: New gems appear at top
- **Combo**: Chain reactions score extra points
- **Special Gems**: Match 4+ creates power-ups

### Power-Ups

**Match 4 (Line)**:
- Creates **Line Blaster**: Clears entire row/column

**Match 5 (L or T shape)**:
- Creates **Color Bomb**: Removes all gems of one color

**Match 5 (Straight line)**:
- Creates **Rainbow Gem**: Combines with any color

**Two Power-Ups Combined**:
- Massive screen-clearing explosions!
- Huge point bonuses
- **The Hook**: Players chase these moments

### Visual Effects (The Pretty Part)

**Particle Systems**:
- Sparkles when matching
- Explosion effects
- Star bursts for combos
- Gem shine/glimmer animations
- Rainbow effects for special gems

**Juicy Feedback**:
- Screen shake on big combos
- Gem wobble animations
- Smooth drop tweening
- Satisfying "pop" on match
- Score numbers float up

**Color Palette**:
- Vibrant, saturated colors
- Gradient fills on gems
- Glow effects
- **Polish**: Everything looks delicious

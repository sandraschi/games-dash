üéÆ Game Flow

  1. Blinds: Small blind ($10) and big blind ($20) posted
  2. Pre-Flop: Each player gets 2 hole cards (face down)
  3. Betting Round 1: Check, call, raise, or fold
  4. Flop: 3 community cards dealt face up
  5. Betting Round 2
  6. Turn: 4th community card dealt
  7. Betting Round 3
  8. River: 5th community card dealt
  9. Betting Round 4
  10. Showdown: Best 5-card hand wins!

üí∞ Actions

  • Fold: Give up your hand, lose any bets made
  • Check: Pass the action (when no bet to call)
  • Call: Match the current bet
  • Raise: Increase the bet (forces others to call, raise, or fold)
  • All-In: Bet all your remaining chips

üìç Position Matters!

  • Early Position: Play tight, only premium hands
  • Middle Position: Can play more hands
  • Late Position (Button): Play widest range, see what others do first
  • Blinds: Already invested, defend with decent hands

üéØ Basic Strategy Tips

  • Play tight and aggressive (fold bad hands, raise good ones)
  • Don't chase draws without proper pot odds
  • Pay attention to betting patterns
  • Position is power - act last when possible
  • Don't play every hand - patience wins
  • Bet sizing matters (don't overbet or underbet)

üìä Pot Odds

Formula: Cost to Call √∑ (Pot + Cost to Call)

Example:

Pot = $100, Bet to call = $20

Pot odds = $20 √∑ ($100 + $20) = $20 √∑ $120 = 16.7%

If your hand has >16.7% chance to win, it's a profitable call!

ü§ñ How Our Poker AI Works

Our AI uses mathematical evaluation and strategic decision-making. Here's the breakdown:

Hand Strength Evaluation

Pre-Flop (Hole Cards Only):

  • Pocket Pairs: Rated 0.5 to 1.0 (AA=1.0, 22=0.57)
  • High Cards: Based on highest card (A=1.0, 2=0.14)
  • Suited Bonus: +0.1 (better flush chances)
  • Connected Bonus: +0.05 (better straight chances)

Post-Flop (With Community Cards):

  • Four of a Kind: 0.95 strength
  • Full House: 0.90 strength
  • Three of a Kind: 0.75 strength
  • Two Pair: 0.65 strength
  • One Pair: 0.40 + (rank/40) - higher pairs = stronger
  • High Card Only: rank/20 (weak)

Decision Logic

Hand Strength AI Action
>70% (Very Strong) Raise 70% / Call 30%
50-70% (Strong) Call
30-50% (Marginal) Check pot odds, call if favorable
<30% (Weak) Fold 80% / Bluff call 20%

Pot Odds Calculation:

AI calculates: Cost to Call √∑ (Pot Size + Cost)

If pot odds < 30% with marginal hand, AI calls. Otherwise folds.

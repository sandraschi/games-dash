üéÆ Basic Rules

Objective: Score the most points by forming words on the board.

Setup

  • 15√ó15 board with premium squares
  • Each player draws 7 tiles from the bag (100 total)
  • First player must use the center star (‚òÖ)

Gameplay

  1. Place tiles to form a word horizontally or vertically
  2. All words formed (including cross-words) must be valid
  3. Words must connect to existing tiles (except first turn)
  4. Calculate score using letter values and premium squares
  5. Draw tiles to refill your rack to 7
  6. Next player's turn

Valid Moves

  • ‚úÖ Words must be in the dictionary
  • ‚úÖ Read left-to-right or top-to-bottom
  • ‚úÖ Minimum 2 letters
  • ‚ùå No proper nouns (Bob, Paris)
  • ‚ùå No abbreviations (Dr., USA)
  • ‚ùå No hyphenated words

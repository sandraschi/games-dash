Viking Strategy Game (The King's Table)

Defenders: Get the King to a corner! Attackers: Capture the King!

The Battlefield

Game Rules

Defenders (Blue): Move the King (Gold) to any corner to win!

Attackers (Red): Surround the King on 4 sides to capture!

Movement: Pieces move like rooks (orthogonal only)

Capture: Sandwiched pieces are captured

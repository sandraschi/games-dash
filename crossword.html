üìñ How to Download Crosswords

Option 1: NYTimes (Subscription Required)

  • Go to nytimes.com/crosswords
  • Log in with your NYTimes subscription
  • Click "Print" on any puzzle to download as PDF
  • Or use browser extensions to export as .puz file

Option 2: XWord Info (Free Puzzles)

  • Visit xwordinfo.com
  • Browse puzzles by date (click any date on calendar)
  • Click "Print" link to get printable version
  • Note: Direct .puz downloads may require account

Option 3: Free Crossword Sites

  • Guardian Crosswords - Free daily puzzles
  • Boatload Puzzles - Free online crosswords
  • Many sites offer "Print" or "Export" options

Supported Formats:

  • .json - Recommended! Easy to create manually
  • .puz - Binary format (requires conversion tools)

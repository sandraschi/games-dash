üÉè How to Play Old Maid

  • Goal: Don't be left with the Old Maid card!
  • Setup: One Queen is removed (becomes the Old Maid)
  • Gameplay: Match pairs and discard them
  • Drawing: Take a card from the next player
  • Matching: Same rank (two 7s, two Kings, etc.)
  • Winner: Last person holding the Old Maid loses!
